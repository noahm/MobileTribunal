Tips
  • The Chat Log may contain some colorful language. Try to distinguish between offensive speech and playful ribbing when reaching a verdict.
  • If you can't understand the written language in the chat log, skip the case. You should not be punishing people for not speaking your native language.
  • Make sure to read through at least a few games to get an accurate picture of the player.
  • If you're not sure, Skip the case. If you're done for now, you can end the session without voting.
  • You will only get credit for the case if you correctly predict the outcome of the case. Punishing every case won't earn you as much IP as thoughtfully judging each case.
  • Being bad at the game isn't a punishable offense. You should be looking at more than the score or item build to determine a verdict.
  • "Pardon" means that you believe the player doesn't deserve to have any action taken against them.
  • "Punish" means that you believe some sort of action should be performed on this player - not necessarily a permaban.
  • An offensive name is a punishable offense. You can determine if the name offends you.
